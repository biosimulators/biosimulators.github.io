1. About BioSimulators¶

1.1. License¶

BioSimulators is released under the MIT License.

1.2. Development team¶

BioSimulators was developed by the Karr Lab at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and the Center for Reproducible Biomedical Modeling.

1.3. Contributing to BioSimulators¶

We enthusiastically welcome contributions to BioSimulators! Please see the guide to contributing and the developer’s code of conduct.

1.4. Acknowledgements¶
